Blackjack

Game Rules

  • Aces (A) count as 11.
  • Face cards (J, Q, K) count as 10.
  • The goal is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over.
  • If you get exactly 21, you have Blackjack!
  • If you go over 21, you lose the game.
